Meet HM3 Bruno

HM3 Bruno is a highly skilled Hospital Corpsman with four years of distinguished military medical experience, demonstrating exceptional leadership, technical proficiency, and clinical expertise across multiple assignments, including HQBN, V 2/8, and V 2/6.

They have managed the medical records of over 565 Marines and provided direct care to more than 325 patients, ensuring accuracy, compliance, and continuity of medical readiness throughout their units. HM3 Bruno has performed over 450 immunizations and blood draws, exhibiting outstanding attention to detail and precision in all clinical procedures.

In addition to their medical and administrative duties, they have trained more than 350 Marines in Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TCCC), ensuring operational readiness and lifesaving capability across the force. Their 1,250+ hours of field care and training underscore a deep commitment to hands-on medical excellence and mission success.

Known for delivering high-quality care in fast-paced, high-pressure environments, HM3 Bruno consistently demonstrates the ability to manage emergent situations with confidence and critical thinking.
