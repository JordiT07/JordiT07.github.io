Page Design

1. Home Page (index.html)

  • Purpose: To introduce visitors to Marco’s Navy Corpsman Website and explain the medical team’s mission within the U.S. Navy and Marine Corps.
  • Audience: General visitors, friends and family, students, and military supporters.
  • Type of Content: Hero image, introductory paragraph, and overview of the site’s purpose.
  • Data Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links: Navigation bar links to About, Gallery, Medical Training, and Contact pages.
  • Notes: Includes a large image and short paragraph describing the Corpsmen’s mission to provide medical care across Navy and Marine units.

2. About Page (about.html)

  • Purpose: To provide detailed background information about HM3 Marco Bruno and his professional experience as a Navy Corpsman.
  • Audience: General public, students, military personnel, and those interested in Navy medical careers.
  • Type of Content: Text biography, descriptions of assignments, and details about Marco’s accomplishments, training, and leadership.
  • Data Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links: Navigation bar to other pages and internal links to related sections like Gallery and Medical Training.
  • Notes: Highlights Marco’s service record, skills, and impact in training over 350 Marines in Tactical Combat Casualty Care (TCCC).

3. Gallery Page (gallery.html)

  • Purpose: To display images of Marco and his squad during various assignments and training sessions.
  • Audience: Friends, family, and military supporters who want to visually experience the Corpsmen’s work.
  • Type of Content: Photo gallery with captions and hover animations using CSS and JavaScript.
  • Data Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links: Clickable thumbnails that enlarge images using a lightbox effect.
  • Notes: Gallery features placeholder images until client-provided photos are uploaded. Designed to use hover animation and jQuery interactivity.

4. Medical Training Page (medical.html)

  • Purpose: To inform users about the Navy Corpsman training pipeline and their responsibilities supporting the Marine Corps.
  • Audience: Visitors interested in learning about military medical training or pursuing Navy careers.
  • Type of Content: Informational text describing the 5-month medical training course and field training program, with external links for more details.
  • Data Fields: None.
  • Validation: N/A.
  • Links: External link to the official U.S. Navy Hospital Corpsman page.
  • Notes: May include embedded videos or infographics in the future to demonstrate field training techniques.

5. Contact Page (contact.html)

  • Purpose: To allow visitors to send messages or appreciation notes to Marco.
  • Audience: General public, friends, family, and supporters of the Navy Corpsman program.
  • Type of Content: Contact form with Name, Email, and Message fields.
  • Data Fields: Yes — Name, Email, and Message fields.
  • Validation: JavaScript ensures all fields are filled and email is formatted correctly before submission.
  • Links: Navigation bar to other pages and footer validation links.
  • Notes: Read-only submission (no stored data) with success confirmation message upon validation.
